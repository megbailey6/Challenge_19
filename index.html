Contact Me

Hello! I'm a Utah-based UX/UI designer with a passion for design and a love for the digital world. In my spare time you can often find me reading, writing, painting, or walking my dog.

My design philosophy is inspired by my experiences working in early childhood education and is rooted in empathy for users of all ages. I believe in creating accessible designs that are simple, memorable, and intuitive.

I thrive when I'm learning new skills and coming up with solutions to challenging problems. I enjoy creating solutions that blend creativity and functionality to create positive digital experiences for users.

Drawing from my experiences in education, I bring a diverse skill set to UX design, including communication, collaboration, and adaptability. This versatility enables me to approach design challenges from multiple angles, resulting in inclusive and innovative solutions.

User Research

I like to conduct user interviews in person whenever possible so that I can see the person's facial expressions. I record interviews (with permission) and both take notes and often use transcription software to refer back to later. I try to ask thoughtful questions to get good data.

User Empathy

I use Google Drive to organize interview notes and Figjam to collaborate with a team to synthesize user data gathered during interviews, such as affinity diagrams and empathy maps.

Qualitative Interviews

During interviews, I try to implement the “Five Whys” method to help get answers that get deeper into the issues I'm trying to learn about. For example, a user says, “I don't like giving gift cards as gifts” (GiftJoy Project), I want to know why and how the app can help them find good alternatives.
